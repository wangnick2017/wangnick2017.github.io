Weijie Wang

I am currently a Ph.D student in Computer Science at Yale, where I am advised by Prof. Charalampos (Babis) Papamanthou.

My research interests lie in applied cryptography and security, especially zero-knowledge proofs and applications on blockchains.

Before coming to Yale, I received my Bachelor's degree in ACM Honors Class of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I worked in Prof. Yu Yu's Crypto-system Laboratory. During my undergraduate, I was also a research intern at TAMU, advised by Prof. Yupeng Zhang.
